3. Teacher Class Assignments Assign Subjects: Assign teachers to specific subjects and classes/sections. For instance, a teacher may be assigned to teach "Mathematics" for Grade 9, or "Biology" for Grade 12. Class Schedule: Maintain the weekly teaching schedule of each teacher, showing the subjects, periods, and classrooms assigned. Class/Section Management: Track which teacher is responsible for each class and section, making it easier to organize and allocate resources.
