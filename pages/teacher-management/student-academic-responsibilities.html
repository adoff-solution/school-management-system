7. Grading and Academic Responsibilities Grade Assignment: Allow teachers to input and manage student grades for assignments, exams, and projects in their assigned subjects. Gradebook: Maintain an online gradebook where teachers can track student performance on different tests and assignments. Report Cards: Generate and print student report cards with subject-wise grades, comments, and performance summaries. Assignments and Homework: Teachers should be able to assign homework or projects to students and track their submission status.
