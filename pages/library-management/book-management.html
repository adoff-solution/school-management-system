1. Book Management Add New Books: The ability to add new books to the system. Each book entry could have: Title Author Publisher ISBN number Year of publication Genre/Category (e.g., fiction, non-fiction, science, etc.) Copies available Shelf location Edit Book Information: Modify the details of existing books (e.g., update the number of copies, change the location). Delete Books: Remove books that are no longer in circulation. Search Books: Search books by title, author, ISBN, category, or other attributes. Book Availability: Display the availability status (whether the book is available for borrowing or not).
