6. Grading and Academic Records Track Grades: Record grades for each subject at the end of each term, exam, or semester. This could include midterm results, final exams, and other assessments. Grade Reports: Generate and print detailed grade reports for individual students. Include subject-wise marks, grade points, and overall GPA. Transcript Generation: The system should allow for generating transcripts, showing the student's complete academic performance over the years. Ranking: Rank students based on their performance in the class, or provide percentile scores.
