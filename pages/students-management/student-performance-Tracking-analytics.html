12. Student Performance Tracking and Analytics Track Performance: Generate detailed analysis of a student's performance over time, including grades, attendance, and extracurricular participation. Graphs/Charts: Visual representations like graphs, pie charts, and bar charts that track trends in academic performance or behavior. Compare Students: Provide functionality to compare student performance against peers or class averages. Identify Struggling Students: Identify students who may be struggling academically based on their grades and attendance, and notify teachers or parents.
